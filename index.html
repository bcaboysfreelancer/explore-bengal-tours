The Journey of Explore Bengal

Built on transparency, traveler comfort, and deeply satisfying holiday management experiences.

Crafting Personalized Vacations with Honesty

Explore Bengal Tours started in 2012 with a single mission: to design authentic, hassle-free holiday itineraries for travelers seeking comfort and transparency. Rather than offering rigid templates, we focus on custom arrangements that cater to specific preferences.

Over the last decade, we have grown into a Govt. Registered tour operating firm, serving more than 15,000 families across pristine Himalayan altitudes, sun-kissed Goan coastlines, backwater houseboats in Kerala, and beautiful coral islands of Andaman.

Our core strengths: no dynamic hidden fees, verified premium hotels with scenic views, customized dining plans, and verified regional guides.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have queries regarding booking terms, payment splits, permits, or travel updates? Find accurate answers instantly.

You can secure your customized trip booking in three easy steps:

  • Finalize your custom itinerary details with our support executive.
  • Pay a standard 30% advance deposit into our official bank account.
  • Receive a verified booking receipt, hotel confirmations, and permit processing status within 24 hours.

Note: The remaining balance is payable on day 1 of the trip during hotel/cab onboarding.

We understand that schedules change due to emergency situations. Our cancellation slabs are highly transparent:

• 30+ days before travel: Full refund of advance deposit minus a ₹1,000 standard processing fee.

• 15 to 30 days before travel: 50% refund of advance deposit.

• Less than 15 days: Non-refundable due to early commitments paid to block premium hotel rooms and transit vehicles.
